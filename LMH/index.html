About L.M.Hill Carpets

Transform your home with beautiful new flooring from L.M. Hill Carpets, based in Hillsborough in Sheffield. "Your local carpet shop." With a wide range of flooring options to choose from, you’ll find the perfect style, comfort level and practicality that’s just right for the way you live. We offer a huge choice of Carpet, Laminate, Vinyl and Rugs, as well as underlay and accessories at prices to suit all budgets.
