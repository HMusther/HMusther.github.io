Cushion Floors

Available in a variety of wood, stone and abstract designs with a range of size options. All our sheet vinyl comes with a heavily cushioned backing, providing a warm and comfortable sensation underfoot. We recommend that all sheet vinyl be fitted onto a solid, clean and smooth sub-floor. The vinyl can be loose laid, fully adhered or semi-adhered using double-sided tape or spray adhesive. If you have any questions, please don't hesitate to contact one of our experienced members of staff, who will gladly help with any fitting advice needed.
