Cushion Floors

Available in a variety of woods, stones and abstract colours with varying size options. All our sheet lino comes with a heavily cushioned backing making for a warm and comfortable sensation under foot. We recommend that all sheet vinyl be fitted onto a solid, clean and smooth sub-floor. The vinyl can be loose laid, fully adhered or semi-adhered using double sided tape or spray adhesive.
